Revenue Recognition

Premium Analysis Guide: AS vs Ind AS 115 vs IFRS 15 vs US GAAP ASC 606

💡

At a Glance: Core Differences

  • AS focuses on the transfer of risks and rewards. Ind AS 115, IFRS 15, and US GAAP ASC 606 use a converged 5-step model focused on the transfer of control.
  • The 5-step model requires unbundling complex contracts into distinct performance obligations, deferring revenue until each is satisfied.
  • Variable consideration (like bonuses or penalties) must be estimated and constrained under the new standards, rather than waiting for certainty.
